Put your MP3 files in THIS folder (same place as fah.mp3).

For the Bruh button to work, the file must be named exactly:

  bruh.mp3

Full path (Windows example):
  ...\brainrot-soundboard\sounds\bruh.mp3

If your download has a different name, either rename it to bruh.mp3
or change the "file" field for Bruh in ../script.js to match.
